Frequently Asked Questions


How do I join?

Simply come to a meeting! We meet every Thursday at 7:30pm. Feel free to join the Discord server too to stay updated.

Do I need to know how to code?

Game Development has many facets such as programming, art, music, testing, and writing. Joining our club will help you with all of those and with no prior experience necessary!

How can I join the eboard?

We hold eboard elections at the end of every Spring semester unless special circumstances arise. We also usually elect a freshman rep in the fall. If you would like to join the eboard, participate often!

Can I host a workshop of my own?

Reach out to us at sbgamedev@gmail.com and we may be able to fit you in sometime! Currently our Fall meetings are pretty filled up so apologies if you have to go in Spring.
